I first got interested in graphic novels when I was in the seventh grade. I loved them so much that I would draw the characters all the time.

Drawing: I feel that I have been an artist all my life. But the more I drew I wanted to tell stories just like the ones I read. And so, I did. I have currently written two unpublished graphic novels.

My Future Plans: I dream to continue writing and drawing my stories as graphic novels. One day I will publish them. I hope that I can inspire other artist and aspiring artist to tell their stories too.
